Chaos and Fear as the Earthquake Struck General Santos City

October 10, 2025 | By News Watch Staff

At around 10:00 a.m. on October 10, 2025, people in General Santos City were doing their usual morning activities when a strong earthquake suddenly hit. In just a few seconds, everyone started to panic as the ground shook hard. People ran out of buildings, schools, and houses to look for a safe place.

Some residents said they first heard a loud rumbling sound before the shaking began. Others thought it was just a passing truck until the tremors got stronger. In schools, teachers guided students to open areas while office workers quickly left their buildings.

Many were scared and confused, not knowing what to do or how long the shaking would last. Cars stopped, electricity flickered, and people screamed as they tried to stay safe. Some even fell to the ground because of the strong movement.

When the earthquake finally stopped, everyone rushed to check their families and friends. People gathered in open spaces, some crying, others just silent and shocked. The quake became known as part of the “Scary 10” because it happened on October 10 at 10 a.m. It was a moment that many in General Santos City will never forget. A mix of fear, chaos, and relief that they survived.
