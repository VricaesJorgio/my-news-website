Magnitude 7.4 Earthquake Strikes The Islands Of Southern Mindanao

October 10, 2025 | By News Watch Staff

A powerful 7.5 magnitude earthquake hit the Davao region on Friday, October 10, 2025, causing heavy damage and forcing many residents to leave their homes and buildings in different parts of Mindanao.

According to the Philippine Institute of Volcanology and Seismology (PHIVOLCS), the center of the quake was found east of Davao de Oro with a depth of about 15 kilometers. The shaking was also felt in nearby areas such as General Santos City and Cotabato.

After the earthquake, rescue teams and government agencies immediately responded to help affected people. Power and communication lines in some places were also cut off for safety reasons. Officials reminded everyone to stay alert because of possible aftershocks.

President Ferdinand Marcos Jr. shared his concern for the people in Davao and said that emergency response teams are already working to give aid and support to those in need.
