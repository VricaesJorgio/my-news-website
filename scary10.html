The Scary 10-10-10: A Morning Mindanao Will Never Forget

October 10, 2025 | By News Watch Staff

The “three 10’s” stands for the unusual coincidence of the earthquake happening on October 10, 2025, at exactly 10:00 in the morning. Many people found it strange and chilling because all the numbers lined up to ten — the 10th day, the 10th month, and the 10th hour. This made the event more memorable and frightening for those who experienced it. The “10-10-10” became a way to describe how shocking and unforgettable that day was for the people of Mindanao.

The 7.4 magnitude earthquake that struck Davao and General Santos City at 10 A.M. on October 10, 2025, shocked residents as buildings trembled and people rushed outdoors seeking safety. The eerie “three tens” coincidence made the event even more unforgettable for many Mindanaoans.
